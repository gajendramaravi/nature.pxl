Chasing the Light

Nature never rushes, yet everything is perfect. The best shots are born in silence. Wait for it • Earn the shot.

Behind the Lens
Nature Photographer · Korba, Chhattisgarh, India

I’m a self-taught mobile photographer from Korba, Chhattisgarh. Photography, for me, is more than a hobby — it’s a quiet connection with nature and the peace hidden within it. I capture moments that hold emotion, memories that bring joy, and the stillness found in the wild.

My journey has taken me from the tribal heartlands and waterfalls of Bastar to the vivid streets of Rajasthan. Using only my phone, I capture landscapes and the quiet stories they carry — preserving moments of stillness, peace, and emotion in every frame.
